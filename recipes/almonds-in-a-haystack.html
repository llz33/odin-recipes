Almonds in a Haystack (1975) Recipe

Description

If you are a person that loves miracle whip, but doesn't know what to do with the five gallon drums that fill the storage room in your basement, this appetizer is just for you!

This recipe is the perfect appetizer for a party with friends or even something to leave out on your kitchen table just in case you want a tantalizing taste for a midnight snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of finely chopped ham
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup of Miracle Whip
  • 1/3 cup of green onion slices
  • 1/4 cup of pickle relish
  • Blue Diamond slivered almonds, toasted

Directions

Step 1

Combine ham, 4 oz. cream cheese, 1/2 cup salad dressing, 1/2 cup toasted slivered almonds (chopped before adding), onion and pickle relish. Mix well and chill. Shape into 1 large or 2 small mounds.

Step 2

Combine 1/4 cup salad dressing and remaining cream cheese; mix well. Frost mound with cream cheese mixture. Chill slightly; cover with toasted almonds.
